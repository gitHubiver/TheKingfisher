BREAKING NEWS

BREAKING NEWS| Southern Luzon State University (SLSU) has suspended its classes and office operations in all campuses on Monday, May 27, due to Severe Tropical Storm (STS) Aghon, the SLSU Human Resource Management Office (HRMO) announced. Meanwhile, other units of vital operations shall render the necessary services to secure university properties.

// report by Karl Jerome del Mundo
